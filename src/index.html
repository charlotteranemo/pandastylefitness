I'm lazy.

Always have been, probably always will be. I like it when things are easy and fun, or atleast can be made easier. I also like pandas. They and I share the same philosphy, eat and sleep are the best things in life. But, even though I am lazy and like taking naps, I have this wish to get into shape. So I created PandaStyleFitness. A page dedicated to losing weight and getting fit - panda style. I can still take naps, I can still eat, I can still be lazy - but while doing that I find ways to incorporate a healthy lifestyle into my life. I won't give up ice cream, I won't quit sugar, but I will however find smart ways for the lazy person to get into shape. Eat, sleep, panda, repeat.
